snowy owl

snowy owl fact file.

Length: 20-27 inches (wingspan of 4.5-5.5 feet).

Weight: 40-70 ounces; males smaller than females.

Lifespan: The oldest snowy owl living in the wild was recorded to be 9 years and 5 months.

Food:Snowy owls mainly eat mammals, ranging from small rodents to large hares.

Habitat:Snowy owls mainly live in the Arctic in open, treeless areas called tundra.
